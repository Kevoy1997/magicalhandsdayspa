Our Story

A Jamaican-owned spa built on passion and care

Established in 2018, Magical Hands Day Spa is a proudly Jamaican-owned business in the heart of Negril. We have certified, knowledgeable specialists who provide a wide variety of spa and beauty services — all at the most affordable prices.

With over 10 years of combined experience in the spa industry, our team is constantly training and staying current with the latest techniques and products. Here at Magical Hands, you are not just a client — you are family.
